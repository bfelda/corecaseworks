Core Caseworks is the manufacturer of the first flush mount computer enclosure. The flush enclosure is intended to be mounted on a wall in a living room or home theater, however it can be installed anywhere there is a surface.

With the advent of the Media Center, many households are seeing their plethera of set top boxes dissappear and being replaced with one Media Center computer. Modern computers have the capabilities of delivering rich web content, such as steaming video and radio, as well as incorporating modern media players such as blue-ray disk players. With this and the ability to wall mount your flat panel TV, the TV stand, or entertainment center is becoming a waste of space.

This is where Core Caseworks flush mount enclosures come in.
