Wall Mount your Media Center
    
 
 
 

Core Caseworks is the manufacturer of the first in-wall computer case.

With the advent of the Media Center, many households are seeing their set top boxes dissappear and being replaced with one Media Center computer. Modern computers have the capabilities of delivering rich web content, such as streaming video and radio, as well as incorporating modern media players such as blue-ray disk players, and HDTV Tuners. With this and the ability to wall mount your flat panel TV, the TV stand is becoming a waste of space.

This is where Core Caseworks Products come in. With the computer case installed in the wall, all wires can be run through the wall creating a very clean appearance. There is enough room inside the case to house your remote control receivers, even with full size ATX components.
